About DealFree

DealFree is India's leading broker-free property platform. We connect property owners directly with buyers and tenants, eliminating unnecessary brokerage fees and making real estate transactions transparent and affordable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the real estate industry in India by providing a platform where property transactions happen directly between owners and seekers, without any middlemen. We believe in transparency, affordability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and preferred property platform, helping millions of people find their dream homes and properties without paying hefty broker commissions.
