Frequently Asked Questions

How does DealFree work?

DealFree connects property owners directly with buyers and tenants without any brokers in between. You can browse listings, contact owners directly, and complete transactions without paying any brokerage fees.

Is there any fee to list my property?

No! Listing your property on DealFree is completely free. You can create a listing, add photos, and connect with potential buyers without any charges.

How do I contact property owners?

Once you find a property you like, simply click on "View Details" and you'll get the owner's contact information. You can call or email them directly to schedule a visit.

Are the listings verified?

Yes, we verify all property listings and owner details before publishing them on our platform to ensure authenticity and reliability.
